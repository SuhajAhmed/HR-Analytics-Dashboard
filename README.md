# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an end-to-end HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to help organizations monitor employee metrics, understand attrition patterns, and support data-driven HR decision-making.  
The dashboard provides clear visibility into employee demographics, attrition trends, and role-based insights.

### **2. Attrition Analysis**
- Attrition by Education  
- Attrition by Salary  
- Attrition by Age Group  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Years at Company  
- Attrition by Gender  

### **3. Department & Job Role Insights**
- Comparison of attrition across job categories  
- Understanding termination patterns  
- Identification of high-risk roles
